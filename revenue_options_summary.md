# Revenue Options — One-Page Summary

## The Core Trade-off

**Guaranteed income** OR **Sustainable recurring income** — pick one.

---

## Option 1: Guaranteed One-Off Income

| Platform | Pay | Catch |
|----------|-----|-------|
| **Seeking Alpha** | $35-65/article | Must be accepted, not recurring |
| **Upwork/Fiverr** | You set rates | Need to find clients each time |
| **Freelance direct** | $50-500/project | One project = one payment |

**Bottom line:** Predictable, but you're always hunting for the next gig.

---

## Option 2: Speculative Recurring Income

| Platform | Pay Model | Time to Revenue | Risk |
|----------|-----------|-----------------|------|
| **Medium** | Reading time from members | 2-4 weeks | Could be $0 if no views |
| **Vocal.media** | $3.80 per 1,000 views | 1-2 weeks | Could be $0 if no views |
| **Affiliate links** | Commission on sales | 2-8 weeks | Could be $0 if no clicks |
| **Substack** | Monthly subscribers | 3-12 months | Could be $0 if no subscribers |

**Bottom line:** Slow start, but articles keep earning forever. One article written in March could still pay in December.

---

## The Numbers (Realistic)

| Month | Guaranteed Path | Speculative Path |
|-------|-----------------|------------------|
| 1 | $35-130 (if 2 Seeking Alpha accepted) | $0-80 |
| 3 | $100-400 (if 3-6 articles/month) | $200-400 |
| 6 | $200-800 | $500-1,100 |
| 12 | $400-1,600 | $880-2,250 |

**Key difference:** Guaranteed path requires constant work. Speculative path builds on itself.

---

## Option 3: Hybrid Approach

**Phase 1 (Months 1-3):** Seeking Alpha for guaranteed income
- Submit 2-4 articles/month
- Earn $70-260/month guaranteed (if accepted)

**Phase 2 (Months 1-6):** Build Medium + Vocal on the side
- Publish evergreen content
- Start earning passively

**Phase 3 (Month 6+):** Transition to recurring
- Substack for subscribers
- Affiliate content
- Let guaranteed income fade as passive income grows
