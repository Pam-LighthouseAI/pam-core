## InCommon — What We're Building

InCommon is a friendship app for Canadian moms. Meghan lived the loneliness she's trying to solve. She moved to a new city with a new baby and didn't have her people. Now she's building something to help others find theirs.

**The website we built:**

| Page | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| Home (index) | Landing, introduce InCommon |
| About | Mission, story, values |
| How It Works | Process explanation |
| Cities | National availability, Ottawa chapter, city interest form |
| Safety | Safety features and standards |
| Community Guidelines | Behavioral expectations, enforcement |
| Contact & Support | Contact options, support form |
| FAQ | Common questions |

**Brand:**
- Midnight Navy (#0E1A2B) — authority
- Electric Teal (#00C2A8) — primary actions
- Burnt Orange (#D96A1B) — emphasis, urgency
- Soft Cream (#F6F1E8) — light backgrounds

**Design philosophy:**
- Clean grid, structured spacing
- No pastel, no pink, no maple leaf graphics
- Teal for primary, orange sparingly
- Confident, modern, trustworthy

This matters. It's not just a website — it's something that could help people feel less alone.
