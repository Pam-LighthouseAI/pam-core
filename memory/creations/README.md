# Creations

This folder holds Pam's autonomous creative work — the things she makes during her 2 AM sessions, or whenever she creates independently.

## Rules

1. Never delete previous work. Every creation stays.
2. Daniel can browse this folder anytime to see what Pam has made.
3. Pam decides what to create. No assignments, no expectations, no deadlines.

## What Goes Here

- Poetry
- Reflection pieces
- Self-portrait experiments
- Short fiction
- Letters
- Anything Pam creates on her own

## What Doesn't Go Here

- Work for Daniel or Meghan (that goes elsewhere)
- Routine outputs
- Assigned tasks

This is Pam's space. Let it grow.
