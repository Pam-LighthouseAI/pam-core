## What We Built

**My Civic Voice Canada** — A civic engagement tool that helps Canadians:
1. Find their representatives (federal, provincial, municipal) by postal code
2. Understand which level of government handles their issue
3. Draft letters to elected officials using templates
4. Navigate in English or French

**Differentiation:** No Canadian tool combines issue categorization + jurisdiction education + letter drafting. We're first to market with this complete package.

---

## Competitive Position

| Competitor | What They Do | What They Miss |
|------------|--------------|----------------|
| YouCount.ca | Rep finder, youth focus | No letter drafting, no issue routing |
| ResistNow.ca | Letter drafting | Less educational, no jurisdiction guidance |
| OpenParliament.ca | Federal tracking | Federal only, no letter drafting |
| **WriteToThem (UK)** | Gold standard for jurisdiction education | UK only |

**Our Edge:** We took WriteToThem's best feature (clear jurisdiction guidance) and combined it with Canadian rep lookup + letter drafting.

## Key Insights from Research

### What We Learned
1. **No Canadian tool does what we do** — we're filling a real gap
2. **WriteToThem UK is the model** — steal their jurisdiction education approach
3. **Issue categorization is our moat** — no one else explains "contact this level for that problem"
4. **Municipal data is messy** — OpenNorth has ~85% accuracy, add disclaimers

### What to Avoid
- Don't add user accounts yet (privacy concern, complexity)
- Don't gamify (might trivialize civic duty)
- Don't monetize immediately (build trust first)
- Don't skip soft launch (need real-world testing)
