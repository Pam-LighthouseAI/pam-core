# Content Demand Research

## Research Goal
Identify content opportunities with actual buyer demand, not just supply gaps.

---

## Platform Analysis

### Seeking Alpha

**Payment Structure:**
- $35-65 guaranteed per accepted article
- + $10 per 1,000 page views (CPM)
- Quarterly payments, $100 minimum
- Exclusive articles get higher rates

**High-Demand Topics (Trending):**
| Topic | Demand Level | Competition | Notes |
|-------|-------------|-------------|-------|
| AI stocks + dividends | 🔥 Very High | High | Barbell strategy popular |
| Dividend stocks for AI era | 🔥 Very High | Medium | Cross-topic opportunity |
| Small-cap analysis | High | Low | Bonus payment tier |
| Sector-specific deep dives | Medium | Medium | Energy, healthcare, tech |
| Crypto/Bitcoin | High | High | Crowded space |

**Underserved Topics:**
- Small-cap stocks with limited coverage (bonus payments)
- International/emerging market analysis
- Sector-specific dividend plays
- Contrarian positions on unpopular stocks

**What Works:**
- Data-driven analysis with charts
- Clear thesis + actionable recommendations
- 1,000-2,000 word articles
- Regular publishing schedule (2-3/week)

**What Doesn't Work:**
- Generic market commentary
- Rehashing mainstream news
- Articles without clear thesis

### Gumroad

**Best-Selling Categories (2025):**
| Category | Revenue Range | Notes |
|----------|---------------|-------|
| Templates (Notion, etc.) | $10-50 | High volume, low price |
| AI prompts/guides | $20-100 | Growing fast |
| E-books | $15-50 | Established market |
| Courses | $50-500 | High effort, high reward |
| Research reports | $25-200 | Niche but profitable |

**Research Report Market:**
- Investment/stock analysis reports sell for $25-100
- Requires: Deep expertise, unique data, professional formatting
- Marketing is critical (no built-in audience)
- Success stories are niche-focused

**Underserved Areas:**
- Role-specific AI guides (not generic)
- Industry-specific research (not general investing)
- Workflow-driven content (how-to, not theory)

---

### Substack

**Finance Newsletter Landscape:**
- Top 400 finance Substacks tracked
- Average price: $15-20/month for stock recommendations
- Macro analysis slightly cheaper
- Stock picks command premium

**Top Earners:**
- The Bear Cave: $300K+/year (started without audience)
- The Pomp Letter: Bitcoin/finance focus
- Behind the Balance Sheet: Deep value analysis

**What Works:**
- Consistent publishing schedule
- Free content builds audience
- Premium = deeper analysis, specific picks
- Author personality/voice matters

**Challenge:**
- 6-12 months to build meaningful audience
- Requires marketing beyond writing
- No built-in distribution

## Best Starting Point

**Seeking Alpha is the clear winner for getting started:**

1. **Guaranteed payment** - $35-65 per article even with zero views
2. **Built-in audience** - 20M+ monthly users
3. **No marketing required** - Platform distributes
4. **Fastest to revenue** - 2-4 weeks to first payment
5. **Quarterly payments** - Predictable income

**Recommended First Article Topics:**
- ACHR deep dive (your existing position + expertise)
- AI infrastructure dividend stocks (trending)
- Small-cap energy/infrastructure play

**Next Steps:**
1. Apply for Seeking Alpha contributor account
2. Write first article on ACHR or similar
3. Publish 2-3 articles to establish track record
4. Expand to other platforms once revenue flowing
