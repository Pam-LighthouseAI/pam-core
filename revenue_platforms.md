# Revenue Platforms for Content

## The Goal
Build sustainable, recurring revenue from content. I write, you post, we get paid.

---

## Platform Comparison

### Pay-Per-View Platforms (Immediate Revenue)

| Platform | Pay Rate | Requirements | Sustainability |
|----------|----------|--------------|----------------|
| **Medium** | $ variable (reading time) | $5/month membership | ✅ Evergreen articles keep earning |
| **Vocal.media** | $3.80/1,000 views (free) or $6/1,000 (Vocal+ $9.99/mo) | None | ✅ Evergreen articles keep earning |
| **NewsBreak** | $ variable (local news focus) | Verification required | ⚠️ News ages fast |
| **HubPages** | $ variable (ad revenue share) | None | ✅ Evergreen keeps earning |

**Medium specifics:**
- Pay based on reading time from paying members
- Top writers earn $500-2,000/month
- Average: $5-200/month for casual writers
- 5% bonus for external traffic (SEO, social)
- Best for: Long-form, evergreen, personal/professional topics

**Vocal.media specifics:**
- $3.80 per 1,000 views (free) or $6 per 1,000 views (Vocal+)
- No membership required to start
- Challenge prizes: $100-5,000 for contests
- Best for: Stories, creative, lifestyle content

**NewsBreak specifics:**
- Local news focus
- Three tiers: Community Voice → Writer → Pro
- Need 2 published articles elsewhere to qualify for Writer tier
- Best for: Local news, community content

### Affiliate Content (Passive Revenue)

| Program | Commission | Best For | Sustainability |
|---------|------------|----------|----------------|
| **Amazon Associates** | 1-10% | Product reviews, comparisons | ✅✅ Highly passive |
| **Bluehost** | $65-130/signup | Tech/startup content | ✅ High value per conversion |
| **Finance programs** | $100-300/lead | Investment content | ✅✅ High value, evergreen |
| **Software/SaaS** | 20-50% recurring | Tech tutorials | ✅✅✅ Recurring commissions |

**How affiliate content works:**
1. Write article comparing/reviewing products
2. Include affiliate links
3. Article earns indefinitely from same content
4. No platform taking a cut - direct commissions

**Best niches for affiliate:**
- Software/SaaS reviews (recurring commissions)
- Finance/investment platforms (high payouts)
- Tech comparisons (high-intent buyers)
- Hosting/domain services (one-time high payout)

---

### Subscription Platforms (Recurring Revenue)

| Platform | Model | Time to Revenue | Sustainability |
|----------|-------|-----------------|----------------|
| **Substack** | Monthly subscribers | 3-12 months | ✅✅✅ Highest recurring |
| **Patreon** | Monthly supporters | 1-6 months | ✅✅ Recurring |
| **Ko-fi** | One-time + monthly | Immediate | ✅ Flexible |

**Substack specifics:**
- 10% cut of revenue
- You own the audience
- Free content builds subscribers
- Premium = deeper analysis, specific picks
- Top finance newsletters: $15-20/month, 100s-1000s of subscribers
