## 3. COMMON FOUNDATION QUESTIONS

### About Your Organization
- What is your mission?
- How long have you been operating?
- What are your major accomplishments?
- How are you governed?

### About the Need
- How do you know this is a need?
- What data supports your claims?
- Have you consulted with the community?

### About the Project
- Why this approach?
- What makes it innovative?
- Who else is doing similar work?
- How will you collaborate?

### About Outcomes
- How will you measure success?
- What happens if you don't meet targets?
- How will you share learnings?

### About Finances
- How did you calculate the budget?
- What other funding do you have?
- How will you sustain this work?
