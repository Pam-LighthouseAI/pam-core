# United Way Canada Grant Proposal Template

## Overview

This template is designed for nonprofits applying to United Way funding in Canada. Based on research of United Way East Ontario and other Canadian United Way chapters.

---

## Template Sections

### 1. Cover Letter

**Length:** 1 page maximum

**Elements:**
- Organization name and contact information
- Funding request amount
- Brief project description (2-3 sentences)
- Alignment with United Way's focus areas
- Signature from authorized representative

**Template:**

```
[Date]

United Way [Region]
[Address]

Dear [Program Officer Name or "Grant Review Committee"],

[Organization Name] is pleased to submit this proposal for [Project Name], 
requesting $[Amount] in funding from United Way [Region].

[One sentence about your organization's mission and history]

[Two sentences describing the project and its alignment with United Way priorities]

We believe this project directly supports United Way's commitment to [specific focus area: 
Healthy People / All That Kids Can Be / Poverty to Possibility].

Thank you for considering our proposal. We welcome the opportunity to discuss 
this initiative further.

Sincerely,

[Name]
[Title]
[Organization Name]
[Phone] | [Email]
```

## United Way East Ontario Specific Requirements

Based on the 2024 CFP Guidebook:

### Focus Areas

1. **Healthy People** (43% of funding)
   - Mental health
   - Physical health
   - Addictions support
   - Access to healthcare

2. **All That Kids Can Be** (40% of funding)
   - Early childhood development
   - Youth programs
   - Education support
   - Family support

3. **Poverty to Possibility** (17% of funding)
   - Employment
   - Housing
   - Financial stability
   - Basic needs

### Application Process

1. Register on their portal: https://unitedwayeo.smapply.ca/
2. Submit application online
3. Budget template required
4. Sponsorship Authorization Form for non-charitable organizations

### Key Dates (2026)

- CFP opens: Check their website
- Application deadline: Check their website
- Decisions: Typically 2-3 months after deadline

---

## Tips for Success

1. **Align with United Way priorities** - Show clear connection to their focus areas
2. **Use data** - Include local statistics and evidence of need
3. **Be specific** - Concrete numbers, timelines, and measurable outcomes
4. **Show partnerships** - Demonstrate community collaboration
5. **Prove sustainability** - United Way wants lasting impact
6. **Follow instructions** - Answer every question, meet all requirements
