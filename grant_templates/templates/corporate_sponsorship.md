## 3. FOLLOW-UP LETTER

### After Submission

**Subject:** Following up on [Project] Sponsorship Request

Dear [Contact Name],

I wanted to follow up on the sponsorship proposal we submitted on [date] for [project/event name].

We're excited about the possibility of partnering with [Company Name] and believe this collaboration would create meaningful impact in our community while providing valuable visibility for your brand.

I'm happy to provide any additional information or meet to discuss how we can customize this partnership to meet your goals.

Please let me know if you have any questions or need further details.

Best regards,

[Name]
[Title]
[Organization]

---

### After Approval

**Subject:** Thank You - [Project] Partnership Confirmed!

Dear [Contact Name],

Thank you so much for [Company Name]'s sponsorship of [project/event]. We're thrilled to have you as a partner!

I'll be in touch shortly with:
- Logo files and branding guidelines
- Timeline for promotional activities
- Details about sponsor recognition

In the meantime, please don't hesitate to reach out with any questions.

We look forward to a successful partnership!

Warm regards,

[Name]

---

### After Decline

**Subject:** Thank You for Considering [Project]

Dear [Contact Name],

Thank you for taking the time to review our sponsorship proposal for [project/event].

While we're disappointed we won't be partnering this time, we understand you receive many worthy requests. We hope you'll keep us in mind for future opportunities.

If you have any feedback on how we might strengthen future proposals, we'd greatly appreciate it.

Thank you again for your consideration.

Best regards,

[Name]
