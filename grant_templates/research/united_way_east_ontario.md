## 💡 SUCCESS FACTORS

### What UW Looks For

| Factor | Why It Matters |
|--------|----------------|
| **Alignment** | Clear fit with focus area priorities |
| **Community need** | Evidence-based need statement |
| **Outcomes focus** | Measurable results, not just activities |
| **Collaboration** | Partnerships and coordination |
| **Sustainability** | Plan beyond UW funding |
| **Financial health** | Sound organizational management |
| **Track record** | Demonstrated ability to deliver |

### Common Mistakes to Avoid

| Mistake | How to Avoid |
|---------|--------------|
| Misalignment with focus areas | Review priorities carefully |
| Weak needs statement | Use local data and community voice |
| Activity-focused not outcome-focused | Lead with what changes |
| No collaboration | Build partnerships |
| Unsustainable model | Plan for long-term |
| Missing documents | Use checklist |
| Late submission | Start early |
