## Essential Grant Proposal Sections

| Section | Length | Purpose |
|---------|--------|---------|
| Cover Letter | 1 page | Introduction, request amount, alignment |
| Executive Summary | 1 page | Overview, target population, outcomes |
| Organization Background | 1-2 pages | Mission, history, credibility |
| Statement of Need | 1-2 pages | Problem, data, gap analysis |
| Project Description | 2-3 pages | Goals, activities, timeline, staffing |
| Outcomes & Evaluation | 1-2 pages | Outputs, outcomes, measurement |
| Budget | 1-2 pages | Expenses, income, narrative |
| Sustainability | 1/2 page | Long-term plan |

## SMART Goals Framework

| Letter | Meaning | Example |
|--------|---------|---------|
| **S** | Specific | "Serve 150 youth in Vanier" |
| **M** | Measurable | "80% will improve grades" |
| **A** | Achievable | Within capacity and resources |
| **R** | Realistic | Based on past performance |
| **T** | Timebound | "By end of school year" |

---

## Common Outputs vs Outcomes

| Type | Definition | Examples |
|------|------------|----------|
| **Outputs** | What you DO | # served, # sessions, # hours |
| **Outcomes** | What CHANGES | Improved grades, reduced isolation |

## Tips for Success

1. ✅ **Align with focus areas** - Show clear connection
2. ✅ **Use local data** - Ottawa-specific statistics
3. ✅ **Be specific** - Concrete numbers and timelines
4. ✅ **Show partnerships** - Demonstrate collaboration
5. ✅ **Prove sustainability** - Long-term impact
6. ✅ **Follow instructions** - Answer every question
